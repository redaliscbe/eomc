About Us

Eye Open Mission Church

Eye Open Mission Church is a Christ-centered, Bible-believing church committed to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ and helping people experience God's love, grace, and transforming power. Our church exists to bring hope to the hopeless, healing to the broken, and spiritual restoration through prayer, worship, and the Word of God.

We welcome individuals and families to grow in faith, experience fellowship, and discover their God-given purpose. Through worship services, prayer meetings, counselling, outreach, and compassionate service, we seek to serve God and the community with love and integrity.

Our Mission

To open the spiritual eyes of people through the Gospel of Jesus Christ and lead them into a transformed life of faith, prayer, and service.
